# Ricochet Robots
[play the game](https://janniselef.github.io/ricochet-robots/) · [more information](https://janniselef.github.io/games/ricochet-robots/)

A solo puzzle similar to Ricochet Robots - slide your robot into the goal! Share maps and replays via tokens, links or QR codes - challenge your friends!

Play the advanced mode: Slide through portals, bounce off diagonal walls and reach the goal!
